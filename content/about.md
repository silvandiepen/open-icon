# About

The **Open Icon Foundation** is a non-profit organization dedicated to revolutionizing the world of icons. Our mission is to standardize icons and make them universally accessible, empowering designers, developers, and creators worldwide to enhance their projects with high-quality icons effortlessly.

## Our Mission

At the Open Icon Foundation, our mission is crystal clear: **Standardize Icons, Empower Creativity**. We believe that icons are the universal language of visual communication, and they should be available to everyone, regardless of their skill level or resources. Our core objectives include:

- **Standardization:** We work tirelessly to create and promote open icon standards that simplify icon usage across various platforms and applications.
- **Accessibility:** We are committed to ensuring that icons are accessible to all, fostering inclusivity and creativity.
- **Education:** We provide resources and tools to educate and inspire designers, developers, and creators about the power of icons.

## What We Do

Our foundation's work encompasses a wide range of activities and initiatives, including:

1. **Icon Standardization:** We develop and maintain open icon standards that streamline icon integration across different software and platforms.
2. **Icon Library:** Our extensive icon library offers a diverse collection of high-quality icons that are free for anyone to use, modify, and distribute.
3. **Icon Tools:** We develop and promote tools and software that simplify the creation, customization, and deployment of icons.

## Why Choose Open Icon Foundation?

- **Icon Standards Pioneers:** We are at the forefront of creating and promoting open icon standards that benefit the entire design and development community.
- **Accessibility Advocates:** Our commitment to accessibility ensures that icons are available to individuals and organizations of all sizes and backgrounds.
- **Educational Resources:** We provide valuable resources, tutorials, and guides to help you harness the full potential of icons in your projects.
